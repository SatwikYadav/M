Special Responsibilities of Leaders and Supervisors

Our leaders and supervisors play a special role in promoting unique culture of US LBM. They are responsible for creating a work environment built and trust and alighned with our culture. Leaders and Supervisors help our society thrive by :

  • Attracting and retaining associates who are a good fit with our culture.
  • Modeling ethical behavior that is consistent with these Standards and our culture.
  • Informing associates about the Standards and our shared philosophies.
  • Empowering associates to make good, ethical decisions that reflect our shared philosophies.
  • Asking for and listening to associates' ideas and concerns.
  • Promptly acting to investigate and/or resolve any suspected or actual illegal, unethical, or inappropriate event reported by an associate.
  • Ensuring associates are working in an environment that is safe and respectful.
  • Actively sharing and adopting best practices across the organization.
